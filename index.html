Yelp Open Dataset
An all-purpose dataset for learning.

The Yelp dataset is a subset of Yelp's businesses, reviews, and user data for use in personal, educational, and academic purposes. It's available as JSON files to be used for various academic purposes.

Dataset Summary

1,223,094 tips by 1,637,138 users
Over 1.2 million business attributes like hours, parking, availability, and ambience
Aggregated check-ins over time for each of the 192,609 businesses

The Dataset Challenge

Yelp has created a challenge for students to use their data in innovative ways and break ground in research.
It's a chance for students to conduct research or analysis on Yelp dataset and share their discoveries with Yelp. For instance, trying to figure out how food trends start or identify the impact of different connections from the local graph, how do user's relationships define their usage patterns? Where are the trend setters eating before it becomes popular? Photo classification, Natural Language Processing & Sentiment Analysis, etc.
